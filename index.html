Things To Do in Riviera Maya

Explore these amazing activities during your free time

Chichen Itza Day Trip

Visit one of the New Seven Wonders of the World, this ancient Mayan city is a must-see archaeological site

Cenote Swimming

Swim in crystal-clear freshwater pools in underground caves. Popular cenotes include Dos Ojos, Gran Cenote, and Cenote Azul

Tulum Ruins

Explore ancient Mayan ruins perched on cliffs overlooking the Caribbean Sea - one of Mexico's most scenic archaeological sites

Snorkeling at Mesoamerican Reef

Discover the second-largest coral reef system in the world. Spot colorful fish, sea turtles, and vibrant coral formations

Xcaret Eco-Archaeological Park

Experience Mexico's natural and cultural heritage with underground rivers, butterfly pavilion, and spectacular evening shows
